## 💡 Key Execution Rules

1. **Protect Morning Blocks**: Your morning block (05:15 AM - 08:15 AM) is your absolute sanctuary. Your phone must be across the room. No emails, no Slack, no notifications.
2. **Office Boundary Discipline**: You must set a hard stop at 06:30 PM. Do not let office tasks bleed into your evening study block. Your career switch depends on this boundary.
3. **The Walking Pad Shield**: If you feel tired, groggy, or anxious in the evening, immediately step onto your walking pad at 4.5 kmph. Physical movement acts as a biological shield against mental fatigue.
