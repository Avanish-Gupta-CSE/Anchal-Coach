## 💡 Key Execution Rules

1. **Protect Focus Blocks**: Turn off phone and social notifications during Deep Work blocks (08:00-10:00 AM, 11:00 AM-01:00 PM, 02:00-03:30 PM).
2. **Buffer Utilization**: If a weekday block is missed due to college/personal commitments, utilize the buffer blocks (10:00-11:00 AM) or weekend catch-up blocks to restore progress.
3. **No Sleep Deprivation**: Maintaining 8+ hours of sleep is critical for long-term algorithmic synthesis and coding cognitive retention. Do not cut sleep blocks!
